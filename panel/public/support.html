Support

IPTVS Player

IPTV service and media are not included.

Support can help with the player, but cannot provide subscriptions, playlists, channels, portal credentials, or access to copyrighted media.

Contact

Email gchofficial@gmail.com. Include the app version, platform, device model, provider type without credentials, steps to reproduce, and the exact on-screen error.

Never email passwords, full playlist URLs, MAC addresses, API keys, pairing codes, signing keys, or unredacted diagnostics. Screenshots should hide all provider and account details.

Before contacting support

  1. Confirm the bundled Demo source works.
  2. Confirm the device date, time, and internet connection are correct.
  3. Retry after fully closing and reopening the app.
  4. Check whether the same source works in its provider's supported client.
  5. Record whether the problem affects Live, Movies, Series, or all sections.
