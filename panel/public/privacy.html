Privacy policy

IPTVS Player · Effective 14 July 2026

IPTVS Player is developed and published by George-Cosmin Hanta. This policy explains what the app and its optional cloud service store, why they store it, and the choices available to you. Questions and privacy requests can be sent to gchofficial@gmail.com.

What the app does

IPTVS Player plays IPTV sources and metadata services that you choose and configure. It does not include an IPTV subscription, channels, playlists, or media. You are responsible for using sources you are authorised to access.

Data kept only on your device

Unless you choose cloud sync, app data stays on your device. This includes:

  • source configurations and credentials;
  • local profiles, favourites, hidden categories, and metadata settings;
  • programme-guide and media caches;
  • playback positions and Continue Watching history; and
  • diagnostic messages, unless you manually copy and send them.

Local credentials and profile data use operating-system secure storage where supported. Cached library data is stored in the app's local database. You can remove local data by deleting it in the app, clearing app storage, or uninstalling the app.

Optional cloud sync

The app works without an account. If you choose the cloud panel and pair a device, the service collects and stores the following data for app functionality and account management:

  • your email address and a generated cloud account identifier;
  • generated paired-device identifiers and device labels;
  • profile names and ordering;
  • source configurations, including provider URLs, usernames, passwords, MAC addresses, playlist URLs, EPG URLs, and user-agent settings;
  • metadata-provider settings, API keys, and PINs;
  • hidden-category settings and favourites; and
  • short-lived pairing codes, which normally expire after ten minutes.

Cloud sync does not upload your playback history, Continue Watching data, programme-guide cache, media cache, or local diagnostics. Cloud data is not used for advertising, marketing, behavioural analytics, or profiling.

Providers and network requests

When you add an IPTV or metadata provider, the app sends the configuration and requests needed for playback or metadata directly to that service. The chosen service may receive your IP address, request headers, credentials, and the content identifiers you request. Those user-directed services are governed by their own privacy and security practices; George-Cosmin Hanta does not operate or control them.

Some user-selected or local IPTV services require unencrypted HTTP. The app permits those connections but does not downgrade an HTTPS URL. Prefer HTTPS providers because HTTP traffic can be read or modified in transit.

Service providers and disclosure

Supabase provides authentication, database hosting, and the infrastructure used by optional cloud sync. It processes cloud data on the developer's behalf. GitHub Pages hosts the public panel, privacy, support, and deletion pages. App stores process installation, purchase-account, and store diagnostics under their own policies.

Personal data is not sold or rented. It may be disclosed when required by law, to protect users or the service, or to service providers acting under instructions necessary to operate the features described above.

Security

Developer-operated cloud traffic uses HTTPS/TLS. Cloud access is restricted using authenticated sessions and database row-level security. Sensitive local configuration uses platform secure storage where available. No system can be guaranteed completely secure, so use unique provider credentials and revoke them if you believe they have been exposed.

Retention and deletion

Cloud account data is retained until you delete individual items or your cloud account. You can permanently delete the account from the signed-in web panel under Account → Delete cloud account. This removes the active account, profiles, source and metadata configurations, favourites, pairings, and paired cloud-device identities. Residual encrypted service backups may remain until the service provider's normal backup-retention period expires.

Account deletion does not remotely erase local data already downloaded to a device. Clear that device's app storage or uninstall the app to remove it. If you cannot access the account, follow the account-deletion instructions or email gchofficial@gmail.com from the account address.

Children

IPTVS Player is not designed for children and is intended for adults. The developer does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Contact the address above if you believe a child has provided cloud-account data.

Changes

This policy may be updated when app features or data practices change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated, and material changes will be reflected in the app-store disclosures.
